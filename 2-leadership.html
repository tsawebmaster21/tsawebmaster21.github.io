Current Council


President

The BASH TSA president is Josh Famous. As our chapter’s president, Josh must run meetings and organize the chapters plans.


Vice-President

The BASH TSA Vice-President is Broderick Cuthill. The job of the Vice-President is to assist the president and help with any tasks that go along with the president.




Secretary

The BASH TSA Secretary is Kate Poe. The job of the Secretary is to keep an accurate description of every topic discussed at meetings and official communication.The secretary also occasionally does tasks for the president upon request.


Treasurer - Chris Angus

The BASH TSA Treasurer is Chris Angus. The job of the Treasurer is to monitor and deal with finances for the chapter. The Treasurer also helps manage fundraising.


Parliamentarian

The BASH TSA Parliamentarian is Asher Hamerick. The job of the Parliamentarian is to make sure that all events during meetings follow Robert’s Rules of Order. The Parliamentarian is also occasionally given tasks by the president.


Reporter

The BASH TSA Reporter is Miranda Famous. The job of the reporter is to keep the chapter informed and aware of our organization’s activities and developments. Also, it is the job of the reporter to keep media up to date on new and current events of the TSA chapter.


Fundraising Chair

The Fundraising Chair for our chapter is run by Natalie Zaleski and Emma Houser. The duties of the fundraising chair is to work and manage all things fundraising related. This includes planning fundraising events and coordinating sponsors.


Membership Chair

The membership chairs for BASH TSA are Tyler Hagmann and Madison Martin. The duty of the membership chairs is to inspire as many potential applicants to take part in TSA. The chairs are also in charge of contacting other chapters in necessary and reaching out in order to maximize participation from students to become involved in TSA.
